Shorthand | Meaning         | Example Before | Example After
+=        | Addition        | x += 5         | x = x + 5
-=        | Subtraction     | x -= 3         | x = x - 3
*=        | Multiplication  | x *= 2         | x = x * 2
/=        | Division        | x /= 4         | x = x / 4
%=        | Modulus(remainder) | x %= 3      | x = x % 3
**=       | Exponentiation  | x **= 2        | x = x ** 2
